ABOUT US

The Most Preferred Hotel in West Africa

Moon Hotels & Suites is the most preferred hotel in West Africa, and it is all about the right mix! Locatwd in the heart of Victoria Island. we offer our clients a perfect blend of business & leisure amenties with dining and recreational options delicately infused in one amazing space. We crown all these with services that meet the highest international standards.

Overlooking the new Moon Atlantic City and Atlantic Ocean, it is just a 10-minute drive to the City Centre and only 45minutes away from the Airport.

Our hotels are designed for your comfort and convenience Your security is our primary concern and you will find our customer care second to none.
